All My Fandoms

I am a huge fan of science fiction and fantasy in a lot of forms. My favorite combination is sci-fi comedy. I blame the combination of my mom being a huge nerd and PBS for this. Let me explain...

Star Trek: The Next Generation (1987 - 1994)

Fandom: Trekkie

It all starts with Star Trek: The Next Generation! My mom always enjoyed Star Trek: The Original Series because that's what she grew up with. Her big brother was a fan and controlled the television, so she had no choice but to watch. But it didn't matter because this started a life long journey into Trekkie life. All my Trekkie love starts with The Next Generation. It continues with Star Trek: Deep Space Nine, Star Trek: Voyager and Star Trek: Enterprise on on TV. All movies with the Star Trek brand is a must see, even the ones that aren't the best because its still Trek.

Doctor Who (1963 - the sun dies or BBC cuts budgets, erases tapes, etc)

Fandom: Whovians

I got the pleasure of watching Doctor Who because of PBS. I don't really know when they started show it but I was pretty young, probably 6 or 7. I watched the Fourth Doctor, Tom Baker in adventures and saw his regeneration to the Fifth Doctor, Peter Davidson. I didn't know what was going on but it was fun! I was completely geeked once the BBC came to its senses and revived the series in 2005. Its still going strong and I love the current Doctor and can't wait for the next series.

Red Dwarf (1988 - who knows?)

Fandom: Smegheads (Which is just a curse word in this show)

This I can blame on PBS too. I was a teenager when KETC started showing this series. It should really be higher on the list because it made me realize that I love the combination of comedy and science fiction. It made me laugh, made me think and call people Smegheads under my breath without them knowing what I was talking about! The perfect series in my opinion and they are still producing episodes. I need to watch stuff with longevity

Buffy: The Vampire Slayer (1997 - 2003)

Fandom: Scoobies

Buffy! Buffy! Buffy! When I was a high school/college student Buffy was life. I couldn't wait till the next episode! I read spoilers, I looked a photos from upcoming episodes, I read fanfiction! It was all about the iconic Chosen One to battle vampires and creatures from the Hellmouth.
